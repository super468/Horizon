## [Chinese Scientists Create Ball Lightning Artificially for First Time](https://www.news.cn/tech/20260416/b487a8967b70495f8fc00364844225d6/c.html) ⭐️ 8.0/10

Chinese scientists at the Shanghai Institute of Optics successfully created ball lightning artificially for the first time, publishing their findings in Nature Photonics on April 16. The experiment confirmed that ball lightning is fundamentally an 'electromagnetic soliton'. This breakthrough resolves a long-standing scientific mystery about the nature of ball lightning and provides crucial experimental validation for a theory that lacked empirical evidence. The findings also offer new insights for fusion energy research and high-energy density physics. The team used strong laser-driven terahertz waves to achieve relativistic-level field strengths, ionizing argon gas into plasma. Energy was trapped through the mechanical balance between optical radiation pressure and thermal pressure, creating an energy sphere approximately 100 micrometers in diameter lasting 100 nanoseconds. Through physical scaling, this corresponds to natural ball lightning tens of centimeters in diameter lasting several seconds.

telegram · zaihuapd · Apr 17, 09:00

**Background**: Ball lightning is a rare and poorly understood atmospheric phenomenon appearing as luminous spherical objects during thunderstorms. The electromagnetic soliton theory suggests it represents a self-contained, stable structure of electromagnetic energy. Solitons are wave solutions that maintain their shape due to a balance between nonlinear and dispersive effects, and they have applications across multiple physics domains including optics and plasma physics.

## [PanicLock: Protect MacBook from Forced TouchID Unlock](https://github.com/paniclock/paniclock/) ⭐️ 7.0/10

PanicLock is a new tool that forces MacBooks to require a password instead of TouchID whenever the lid is closed, responding to a real case where Washington Post reporter Hannah Natanson was compelled to provide her fingerprint to unlock her computer, exposing Signal messages and sources. This matters because law enforcement can compel biometric unlock in ways they cannot with passwords, creating a legal vulnerability. The Fifth Amendment has been interpreted to allow compelled fingerprint decryption but not compelled password entry, making this a critical privacy protection tool for journalists and sensitive users. PanicLock modifies macOS login behavior to require password after lid closure. Users can also achieve similar results with a one-line Terminal command: sudo bioutil -ws -u 0; sleep 1; sudo bioutil -ws -u 1. The tool responds to the D.C. Circuit ruling that compelled biometric unlock sits at the intersection of physical-trait and act-of-production precedents.

hackernews · seanieb · Apr 17, 16:38

**Background**: The Fifth Amendment protects against self-incrimination but courts have ruled that fingerprints are physical evidence like DNA, so they can be compelled, while passwords are considered testimonial and cannot be forced. In the Natanson case, FBI agents compelled her fingerprint to access Signal messages on her MacBook, highlighting the legal asymmetry between biometric and password compulsion.

## [VC Giants Accused of Converting Scientists into AI Gig Workers](https://www.thenation.com/article/society/ai-silicon-valley-andreesen-thiel-stem/) ⭐️ 7.0/10

Silicon Valley venture capital figures including Peter Thiel and Marc Andreessen are accused of using political influence to push cuts to federal research funding at NSF and NIH, resulting in over 10,000 STEM PhDs leaving federal employment last year and seeking work on VC-backed AI platforms like Mercor and ScaleAI. This represents a systemic hollowing out of America's public research infrastructure, turning elite scientists into cheap labor for AI companies while undermining decades of federal investment in fundamental research that has historically driven innovation. While Mercor claims to pay $40-150/hr making it one of the better-paying AI gig platforms, critics argue researchers are using advanced academic expertise to train commercial AI models at rates far below the value of their specialized knowledge, effectively subsidizing tech companies through public research destruction.

## [Ancient DNA Reveals Widespread Directional Selection in Recent Human Evolution](https://www.nature.com/articles/s41586-026-10358-1) ⭐️ 7.0/10

Harvard researchers analyzed 15,836 ancient West Eurasian genomes (including 10,016 new samples) and found that directional selection was widespread in humans over the past 10,000 years, challenging the traditional view that such selection was rare in recent human evolution. This is a major finding because it demonstrates that Darwinian natural selection actively shaped modern human complex traits, including body fat percentage, cognitive performance, and schizophrenia risk, rather than being a negligible force in recent evolution. The team estimated selection coefficients for 9.7 million variant sites and found hundreds of alleles under strong selection. The allele frequency changes predicted decreased body fat and schizophrenia risk, while increasing cognitive performance metrics.
