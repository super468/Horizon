## [Axios NPM Supply Chain Attack Drops Cross-Platform RAT](https://www.stepsecurity.io/blog/axios-compromised-on-npm-malicious-versions-drop-remote-access-trojan) ⭐️ 8.0/10

On March 31, 2026, malicious versions v1.14.1 and v0.30.4 of the popular Axios HTTP library were published to npm, containing a fake dependency 'plain-crypto-js@4.2.1' with a postinstall script that deploys a cross-platform remote access trojan targeting Windows, macOS, and Linux. This represents a significant supply chain attack on one of the most widely-used libraries in the JavaScript ecosystem, potentially exposing countless projects to remote access compromise, credential theft, and secret exfiltration. The attack compromised an axios maintainer's npm account to publish the malicious versions. Neither version contains malicious code inside axios itself—the payload is delivered entirely through the fake dependency's postinstall script. Any workflow that installed these versions with scripts enabled may have exposed all injected secrets (cloud keys, repo deploy keys, npm tokens) to an interactive attacker.

hackernews · mtud · Mar 31, 02:54

**Background**: Axios is one of the most popular HTTP client libraries in the JavaScript ecosystem, used by millions of projects for making HTTP requests. The attack exploited npm's trust model by compromising a maintainer account and publishing malicious versions. The postinstall script is a lifecycle hook that runs automatically after package installation, making it a common vector for supply chain attacks.

## [Google Quantum Team Reduces Bitcoin Attack Threshold 20x](https://research.google/blog/safeguarding-cryptocurrency-by-disclosing-quantum-vulnerabilities-responsibly/) ⭐️ 8.0/10

Google's Quantum AI team published a whitepaper optimizing Shor's algorithm to attack Bitcoin's elliptic curve cryptography, reducing required physical qubits from ~10 million to ~500,000 (20x reduction), enabling potential private key recovery in about 9 minutes with 41% success rate. 这标志着量子威胁模型的重大更新。约690万枚比特币（占总供应量的三分之一）因公钥暴露而面临风险，2021年的Taproot升级可能进一步扩大了漏洞范围。 The team compiled two attack circuits requiring fewer than 1200 and 1450 logical qubits. On superconducting quantum computers, both circuits can complete computation in minutes with fewer than 500,000 physical qubits. Attackers could complete most preparation in advance and crack private keys within about 9 minutes after Bitcoin transactions are broadcast.

telegram · zaihuapd · Mar 31, 08:03

**Background**: Shor's algorithm, developed by mathematician Peter Shor in 1994, is a quantum algorithm capable of factoring large integers in polynomial time, threatening elliptic curve cryptography used in Bitcoin. Current quantum computers require quantum error correction, where multiple physical qubits combine to form a single error-tolerant logical qubit. The 2021 Bitcoin Taproot upgrade, while improving privacy and efficiency, inadvertently made public keys visible for Taproot-type transactions, potentially expanding the attack surface.
